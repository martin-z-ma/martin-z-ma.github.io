### Biography
Dr. Martin Z. Ma is the Principal Investigator of the A-SMART Lab and an applied researcher specializing in systems modeling, optimization, machine learning, and process engineering. He has authored numerous peer-reviewed publications and contributed to the development of advanced modeling platforms used across academia, national laboratories, and industry.
